## Altruism Country Deep Dive – Liberia (LR)

> **Status**: First-pass scaffold with approximate ranges (**\***).

### 1. Macro Context

- Population: ~5.3M\*
- GDP (nominal): ~USD 3–4B/year\*
- GDP per capita: ~USD 600–800/year\*
- Low-income West African coastal country with post-war reconstruction and Ebola legacy.

### 2. Government Social Spending and Fiscal Flows

Approximate:

- Total government expenditure: ~28–32% of GDP\* (ODA-supported).
- Social spending (health, education, social protection): ~12–14% of GDP\*.
  - Health: ~4–5% of GDP\*.
  - Education: ~4–5% of GDP\*.
  - Social protection: ~2–3% of GDP\*.

### 3. ODA – Heavy Recipient

- ODA inflows: several hundred million USD/year\*.
- Focus:
  - Health (Ebola recovery, HIV, malaria, maternal/child health).
  - Education.
  - Governance and rule of law.

### 4. Private Philanthropy and Informal Altruism

Formal:

- NGOs and faith-based organizations.

Informal:

- Family and community support.
- Diaspora remittances from Liberians abroad (especially US).

### 5. Cause-Area Context

Burden:

- Under‑5 mortality: ~70–90 per 1,000\*.
  - Neonatal disorders.
  - Malaria.
  - Diarrhea and pneumonia.
- Maternal mortality very high.
- Infectious diseases, plus conflict and disaster legacies.

### 6. Cluster Scenarios & Triads

Clusters:

- Liberian diaspora.

Triads:

- **Mano River Health and Stability Triad**:
  - Liberia, Sierra Leone, Guinea region.
