## Altruism Country Deep Dive – Panama (PA)

> **Status**: First-pass scaffold with approximate ranges (**\***). To be refined with Panamanian budget, financial, and health data.

### 1. Macro Context

- Population: ~4.5M\*
- GDP (nominal): ~USD 70–80B/year\*
- GDP per capita: ~USD 15,000–18,000/year\*
- Upper-middle-income Central American country, major logistics and financial hub centred on the Panama Canal.

### 2. Government Social Spending and Fiscal Flows

Approximate:

- Total government expenditure: ~25–30% of GDP\*.
- Social spending (health, education, social protection): ~12–15% of GDP\*.
  - Health: ~6–7% of GDP\*.
  - Education: ~4–5% of GDP\*.
  - Social protection: ~3–4% of GDP\*.

### 3. Financial Centre / Tax Haven Role

Panama has:

- A long history as an offshore financial centre:
  - Shell companies.
  - Secrecy jurisdictions (e.g. “Panama Papers”).
- Hosts:
  - Large volumes of international capital and legal entities relative to domestic economy.

This implies:

- Significant **potential** for altruism by:
  - Leveraging even tiny levies or voluntary commitments on managed assets.

### 4. Private Philanthropy and Informal Altruism

Formal:

- Some wealthy families and corporates engage in philanthropy.

Informal:

- Family and community support.
- Church and NGO activities.

### 5. Cause-Area Context

Domestic:

- Leading causes of death:
  - NCDs (CVD, diabetes, cancers).
  - Road injuries.
  - Violence in certain areas.
- Child mortality:
  - Under‑5 mortality ~15–20/1,000\*; with pockets of higher rates in indigenous and rural communities.

Regional context:

- Central American corridor faces:
  - Violence.
  - Migration crises.
  - Climate vulnerability.

### 6. Cluster Scenarios

Clusters:

- Offshore clients using Panamanian structures.
- High-net-worth Panamanians.

If:

- Passive altruistic mechanisms such as:
  - Default 0.05–0.1% of AUM annually to CESET funds.

were implemented, then:

- Even with modest levels of assets, flows could reach **hundreds of millions to billions** of USD/year.

Targets:

- Domestic inequality and health.
- Regional CESET causes in Central America and Caribbean.

### 7. NSS/Triad Considerations

Potential Triads:

- **Canal & Corridor Responsibility Triad**:
  - Panama as a logistics and financial hub underwriting regional human security.
- **Offshore Altruism Triad**:
  - Shared across multiple tax havens (Panama, Belize, Cayman, etc.).
