## Altruism Country Deep Dive – Netherlands (NL)

> **Status**: First-pass scaffold with approximate ranges (**\***).

### 1. Macro Context

- Population: ~18M\*
- GDP (nominal): ~USD 1.0–1.1T/year\*
- GDP per capita: ~USD 55,000–60,000/year\*
- Core EU, high-income, very open economy with large trade and financial sectors.

### 2. Government Social Spending and Fiscal Flows

Approximate:

- Total social spending: ~22–24% of GDP\*.
  - Pensions: ~7–8% of GDP\*.
  - Health: ~9–10% of GDP\*.
  - Other social protection: ~5–6% of GDP\*.

Other:

- Education: ~5–6% of GDP.

### 3. Official Development Assistance (ODA)

- ODA/GNI: historically near 0.7%; recently ~0.5–0.7%\*.
- ODA volume: ~USD 5–7B/year\*.
- Focus:
  - Global health and development.
  - Water management.
  - Human rights and governance.

### 4. Private Philanthropy

Formal:

- Private giving ~0.7–1.0% of GDP\*.
- Strong development NGOs and foundations (e.g. Oxfam Novib).

Informal:

- Community and church-based giving.

### 5. Cause-Area Context

Domestic:

- Leading causes of death:
  - CVD.
  - Cancers.
  - Dementia.

International:

- Dutch-origin flows are important for:
  - Development, especially in former colonies and partner countries.
  - Innovative finance (blended finance, impact investing).

### 6. Cluster Scenarios & Triads

Clusters:

- Pension funds and institutional investors.
- High-income households.

Triads:

- **Water and Climate Adaptation Triad**.
- **Innovative Development Finance Triad**.
