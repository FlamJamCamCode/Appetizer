## Altruism Country Deep Dive – Spain (ES)

> **Status**: First-pass scaffold with approximate ranges (**\***).

### 1. Macro Context

- Population: ~47M\*
- GDP (nominal): ~USD 1.6–1.7T/year\*
- GDP per capita: ~USD 34,000–37,000/year\*
- Core EU welfare state with strong regional diversity and post-crisis recovery dynamics.

### 2. Government Social Spending and Fiscal Flows

Approximate:

- Total social spending: ~24–26% of GDP\*.
  - Pensions: ~11–12% of GDP\*.
  - Health: ~7–8% of GDP\*.
  - Other social protection: ~5–6% of GDP\*.

Other:

- Education: ~4.5–5% of GDP.

### 3. Official Development Assistance (ODA)

- ODA/GNI: ~0.2–0.3%\*.
- ODA volume: ~USD 3–5B/year\*.
- Focus:
  - Latin America.
  - North and West Africa.
  - Multilateral funds.

### 4. Private Philanthropy and Informal Altruism

Formal:

- Private giving ~0.3–0.5% of GDP\*.
- Foundations and Catholic/faith-based charities active.

Informal:

- Family support norms strong, especially intergenerational.

### 5. Cause-Area Context

Domestic:

- Leading causes:
  - CVD.
  - Cancers.
  - Dementia.
- Very low child mortality.

International:

- Spain-origin altruism important for:
  - Latin American development and health.
  - Migration and refugee support in the Mediterranean.

### 6. Cluster Scenarios & Triads

Clusters:

- High-income professionals.
- Diaspora ties to Latin America.

Triads:

- **Ibero-American Development Triad**.
- **Mediterranean Migration Triad**.
