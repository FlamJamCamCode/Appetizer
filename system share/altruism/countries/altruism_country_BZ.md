## Altruism Country Deep Dive – Belize (BZ)

> **Status**: First-pass scaffold with approximate ranges (**\***). To be refined with Belizean budget, philanthropy, and health data.

### 1. Macro Context

- Population: ~0.4M\*
- GDP (nominal): ~USD 2–3B/year\*
- GDP per capita: ~USD 5,000–7,000/year\*
- Small lower-middle-income Caribbean/Central American state, historically a financial/offshore centre with tourism and agriculture.

### 2. Government Social Spending and Fiscal Flows

Approximate:

- Total government expenditure: ~30–35% of GDP\*.
- Social spending (health, education, social protection): ~12–15% of GDP\*.
  - Health: ~4–5% of GDP\*.
  - Education: ~6–7% of GDP\*.
  - Social protection: ~2–3% of GDP\*.

Debt and fiscal space are often tight.

### 3. Financial Centre / Tax Haven Role

Belize has:

- Played a role as an offshore financial centre and low-tax jurisdiction.
- Attracted:
  - International business companies.
  - Some wealth-holding structures.

Recent international pressure has:

- Led to partial reforms and transparency improvements.

### 4. Private Philanthropy and Informal Altruism

Formal:

- Domestic philanthropy small but present (environmental NGOs, community foundations).

Informal:

- Strong family, community, and church-based support.
- Diaspora remittances from Belizeans abroad (US, UK, etc.) significant for household welfare.

### 5. Cause-Area Context

Domestic:

- Leading causes of death:
  - NCDs (CVD, diabetes).
  - Injuries (road accidents, violence).
- Child mortality:
  - Under‑5 mortality ~14–20/1,000\*; dominated by neonatal causes and remaining infections.

Environmental vulnerability:

- Hurricanes and climate change impact coastal communities and ecosystems (reefs, mangroves).

### 6. Cluster Scenarios

Clusters:

- Offshored wealth structures and international clients.
- Belizean diaspora.

Scenarios:

- If:
  - A small “solidarity levy” of 0.1–0.2% on assets managed in Belizean structures\* were devoted to CESET-oriented funds:
  - Could mobilize tens to hundreds of millions of USD/year, depending on actual AUM.

Targets:

- Domestic:
  - Health, education, climate resilience.
- International:
  - High-burden neighbouring countries in Central America and the Caribbean.

### 7. NSS/Triad Considerations

Potential Triads:

- **Caribbean Climate and Health Triad**:
  - Belize as a node combining reef protection, hurricane resilience, and health.
- **Offshore Responsibility Triad**:
  - Turning tax haven structures into systematic altruism channels.
