## Altruism Country Deep Dive – Türkiye (TR)

> **Status**: First-pass scaffold with approximate ranges (**\***). To be refined with Turkish budget, aid, and philanthropy data.

### 1. Macro Context

- Population: ~86M\*
- GDP (nominal): ~USD 900–1,000B/year\* (subject to volatility and FX swings).
- GDP per capita: ~USD 10,000–12,000/year\*.
- Upper-middle-income country straddling Europe and Asia with Islamic cultural roots and secular political history.

### 2. Government Social Spending and Fiscal Flows

Approximate:

- Total government expenditure: ~35–40% of GDP\*.
- Social spending (health, pensions, social protection): ~15–20% of GDP\*.
  - Health: ~4–5% of GDP\*.
  - Pensions and social security: ~8–10% of GDP\*.
  - Other social programmes and subsidies: ~3–5% of GDP\*.

### 3. International Aid and Humanitarian Role

Türkiye is:

- A significant humanitarian actor:
  - Hosts large refugee populations (notably Syrians).
  - Provides aid and development support to Muslim-majority and other countries.

ODA-like flows:

- ODA/GNI around 0.8–1.0%\* if counting refugee hosting and certain official flows.

### 4. Private Philanthropy and Religious Giving

Zakat and charitable foundations:

- Religious giving is substantial, both:
  - Informally.
  - Via waqf-style foundations and NGOs.

Formal philanthropy:

- Turkish NGOs active in:
  - Humanitarian assistance abroad (e.g. IHH).
  - Social services domestically.

### 5. Volunteering and Informal Altruism

- Strong culture of:
  - Helping neighbors.
  - Providing hospitality.
  - Supporting the poor during Ramadan and other religious periods.

### 6. Cause-Area Context

Domestic:

- Leading causes of death:
  - NCDs (CVD, cancers).
  - Injuries (road traffic).
  - Some regional disparities in child/maternal mortality.

International:

- Through hosting refugees and providing humanitarian aid, Türkiye plays a major role in:
  - Conflict-related CESET relief.
  - Regional stability.

### 7. Cluster Scenarios

Clusters:

- High-income households and business elites.
- Large Turkish diaspora in Europe (especially Germany).

If:

- 3M diaspora Turks\* and 2M high-income domestic Turks\* each contributed an extra USD 300/year:
  - ~USD 1.5B/year additional flows.

If directed through:

- Well-governed Triads for:
  - Refugee health and education.
  - Regional reconstruction.

This could:

- Significantly reduce suffering in conflict-adjacent regions.

### 8. NSS/Triad Considerations

Türkiye is a natural host for:

- **Refugee and Migration Triads**:
  - Ensuring refugees achieve education, health, and livelihoods.
- **Ummah Humanitarian Triads**:
  - Coordinating Islamic charity and state aid in nearby crisis zones.
