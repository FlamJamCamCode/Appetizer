## Altruism Country Deep Dive – Luxembourg (LU)

> **Status**: First-pass scaffold with approximate ranges (**\***). To be refined with Luxembourg budget, ODA, and financial-centre data.

### 1. Macro Context

- Population: ~0.65M\*
- GDP (nominal): ~USD 80–90B/year\*.
- GDP per capita: ~USD 120,000–140,000/year\* (one of the highest in the world).
- EU member state, major financial centre and fund domicile.

### 2. Government Social Spending and Fiscal Flows

Approximate:

- Total social spending: ~22–24% of GDP\*.
  - Pensions: ~9–10% of GDP\*.
  - Health: ~6–7% of GDP\*.
  - Other social protection: ~6–7% of GDP\*.

Other:

- Education: ~5% of GDP.
- Public finances generally strong.

### 3. Official Development Assistance (ODA)

Luxembourg is:

- Among the few countries that consistently exceed the **0.7% ODA/GNI** target.
- ODA/GNI: typically ~0.9–1.0%\*.
- ODA volume: ~USD 0.4–0.6B/year\*.

### 4. Financial Centre / Tax Haven Role

Luxembourg:

- Is a key domicile for:
  - Investment funds.
  - Multinational tax planning.
- Very large **assets under management** relative to its size.

This makes Luxembourg:

- A crucial node for potential **financial-system-embedded altruism**.

### 5. Private Philanthropy and Informal Altruism

Formal:

- Small but significant philanthropy sector.
- Some foundations and corporate giving.

Informal:

- Community and church-based giving.

### 6. Cause-Area Context

Domestic:

- Leading causes of death like other high-income countries:
  - CVD.
  - Cancers.
  - Dementia.

International:

- Via ODA and financial system influence, Luxembourg contributes to:
  - Development, global health, and climate financing.

### 7. Cluster Scenarios

Clusters:

- Funds and wealth under management.

If:

- Funds domiciled in Luxembourg adopt:
  - A 0.01–0.05% of AUM/year altruistic commitment.

Then:

- Flows to global CESET interventions could reach **multi-billion USD/year**, far exceeding official ODA.

### 8. NSS/Triad Considerations

Potential Triads:

- **Luxembourg Global Fund Triad**:
  - Central platform for global altruistic commitments from financial products.
- **EU Financial-Altruism Triad**:
  - With Luxembourg as a key hub in an EU-wide architecture for embedding altruism in finance.
