## Altruism Country Deep Dive – Burkina Faso (BF)

> **Status**: First-pass scaffold with approximate ranges (**\***). To be refined with Burkinabè budget, aid, and health data.

### 1. Macro Context

- Population: ~22M\*
- GDP (nominal): ~USD 19–21B/year\*
- GDP per capita: ~USD 800–1,000/year\*
- Low-income Sahel country with recent coups, conflict, and substantial rural poverty.

### 2. Government Social Spending and Fiscal Flows

Approximate:

- Total government expenditure: ~25–30% of GDP\* (bolstered by aid).
- Social spending (health, education, social protection): ~10–12% of GDP\*.
  - Health: ~4–5% of GDP\*.
  - Education: ~4–5% of GDP\*.
  - Social protection: relatively limited.

### 3. ODA – Heavy Recipient

- ODA inflows substantial, often **>10% of GNI**\*.
- Donors:
  - Multilaterals, EU, France, other bilaterals.

Support focuses on:

- Health (particularly child and maternal).
- Education.
- Food security, agriculture.
- Governance and security.

### 4. Private Philanthropy and Informal Altruism

Formal:

- Limited domestic philanthropic infrastructure.

Informal:

- Extended family and community mutual aid.
- Religious giving.

### 5. Cause-Area Context

Under‑5 mortality and causes:

- Very high under‑5 mortality (on the order of 80–100 per 1,000 live births\*).
  - Neonatal disorders.
  - Malaria.
  - Diarrhea and pneumonia.
  - Malnutrition.

Adult burdens:

- Infectious diseases.
- Maternal mortality.
- Increasing NCDs and injuries.

Security context:

- Conflict-related deaths and displacement affect:
  - Direct mortality.
  - Indirect mortality via food insecurity and disrupted services.

### 6. Cluster Scenarios

External clusters:

- Sahel-focused philanthropy and ODA.
- Global health donors.

Example scenario:

- If Burkina Faso received an extra **USD 500M/year** targeted at:
  - Child survival package.
  - Maternal health.
  - Nutrition and food security.

Given cost per death averted (~USD 3,000–10,000\*), this could:

- Prevent tens of thousands of child deaths per year, depending on coverage and implementation.

### 7. NSS/Triad Considerations

Burkina Faso is integral to:

- **Sahel Child Survival and Nutrition Triads**.
- **Sahel Stability and Resilience Triads**:
  - Combining peacebuilding with service provision.
