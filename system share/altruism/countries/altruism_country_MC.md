## Altruism Country Deep Dive – Monaco (MC)

> **Status**: First-pass scaffold with approximate ranges (**\***). To be refined with Monaco economic, financial, and philanthropy data.

### 1. Macro Context

- Population: ~40k\*
- GDP (nominal): ~USD 7–8B/year\* (very rough; Monaco publishes limited GDP data).
- GDP per capita: commonly cited >USD 170,000/year\*.
- Microstate on the French Riviera, extreme concentration of ultra-high-net-worth individuals (UHNWI); de facto tax haven.

### 2. Government Spending and Social Structure

Monaco:

- Has a small state apparatus.
- Residents often:
  - Purchase private health and education services.
  - Rely on French systems through bilateral agreements and private arrangements.

Public spending:

- High per capita in:
  - Infrastructure.
  - Security.
  - Public amenities.

### 3. Tax Haven Role

Monaco:

- No personal income tax for residents (with some exceptions).
- Attractive to HNWIs and UHNWI seeking low-tax residency.

This implies:

- A very high concentration of private wealth relative to the tiny population.

### 4. Private Philanthropy

- Many residents engage in:
  - High-profile philanthropy (foundations, donations).
- The principality hosts:
  - Charitable galas.
  - Environmental and ocean-related initiatives (e.g. Prince Albert II of Monaco Foundation).

Global impact:

- Depends heavily on individual choices of wealthy residents and their family offices.

### 5. Cause-Area Context

Domestic:

- Small, high-income, low-burden on most CESET metrics.

Global:

- The key variable is:
  - The fraction of resident wealth directed toward altruistic uses for:
    - Global health.
    - Climate and oceans.
    - Animal welfare.
    - Aging research, etc.

### 6. Cluster Scenarios

Clusters:

- UHNWI residents and their foundations.

If:

- 1,000 Monaco-based UHNWI\* with average wealth USD 100M each committed 1–2% of wealth annually:
  - 1%: 1,000 × 0.01 × 100M = **USD 1B/year**.
  - 2%: **USD 2B/year**.

These flows would:

- Greatly exceed any domestic need.
- Be available for purely global CESET work.

### 7. NSS/Triad Considerations

Potential Triads:

- **Monaco Ocean & Climate Triad**:
  - Given existing environmental focus, could be expanded into a major global node.
- **UHNWI “Heaven Triad”**:
  - Monaco as a demonstration of a wealthy enclave voluntarily committing to serious CESET-focused altruism.
