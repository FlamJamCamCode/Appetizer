## Altruism Country Deep Dive – France (FR)

> **Status**: First-pass scaffold with approximate ranges (**\***).

### 1. Macro Context

- Population: ~68M\*
- GDP (nominal): ~USD 3.0–3.2T/year\*
- GDP per capita: ~USD 44,000–48,000/year\*
- Core EU welfare state with large public sector and global influence.

### 2. Government Social Spending and Fiscal Flows

Approximate:

- Total social spending: ~28–31% of GDP\* (one of the highest).
  - Pensions: ~14–15% of GDP\*.
  - Health: ~9–10% of GDP\*.
  - Other social protection (unemployment, family benefits, housing): ~5–6% of GDP\*.

Other:

- Education: ~5% of GDP.
- Defense: ~1.9–2.1% of GDP.

### 3. Official Development Assistance (ODA)

- ODA/GNI: ~0.5–0.6%\* (below 0.7% target but rising at times).
- ODA volume: ~USD 15–20B/year\*.
- Focus areas:
  - Francophone Africa.
  - Climate and environment.
  - Governance and human rights.

### 4. Private Philanthropy and Informal Altruism

Formal:

- Private giving ~0.3–0.6% of GDP\*.
- Strong foundation and association sector.

Informal:

- Family support.
- Trade-union and association-based solidarity.

### 5. Cause-Area Context

Domestic:

- Leading causes of death:
  - CVD.
  - Cancers.
  - Dementia.
- Very low child mortality; remaining CESETs mostly NCD and mental health.

International:

- France-origin altruism (ODA + philanthropy) strongly shapes:
  - West and Central African development and health.

### 6. Cluster Scenarios & Triads

Clusters:

- High-income professionals.
- Wealthy families and dynasties (industrial, luxury sectors).

Triads:

- **Francophone Africa Child Survival Triad**.
- **Climate and Sahel Stability Triad**.
