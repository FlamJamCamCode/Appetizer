# Aejectivity: Complete Definition and Applications

## I. Core Definition

### A. The Hierarchy of Truth

**Subjectivity:**
- What ONE person experiences (individual)
- "I think this is beautiful" (only you)
- "I believe in God" (personal faith)
- **No agreement needed** (purely individual)

**Aejectivity:**
- What a GROUP shares/experiences together (inter-subjective)
- "WE think this is beautiful" (shared aesthetic, objective within group)
- "WE believe in God" (shared faith, objective within community)
- **Agreement within group** (inter-subjective consistency)

**Objectivity:**
- What EVERYONE experiences/agrees upon (universal)
- "This rock is hard" (verifiable by all, universal agreement)
- "2+2=4" (mathematical truth, universal)
- **Agreement across ALL groups** (universal consistency)

---

### B. The Key Insight

**Most "truths" are aejective, not objective:**
- Religious truths (Christians: "Jesus is risen" - objective to them, subjective to others)
- Cultural values (Japanese: "Harmony is paramount" - objective to them, subjective to individualist cultures)
- Historical narratives (Mainstream: "Holocaust 6M" - objective to mainstream, disputed by revisionists)
- Aesthetic judgments (Ultra-aesthetic Triad: "This is beautiful" - objective to them, subjective to others)

**Aejectivity = Objectivity WITHIN group, subjectivity BETWEEN groups**

---

## II. Aejectivity in AR (Augmented Reality) Context

### A. Shared Hallucinations

**The mechanism:**
- AR system creates visual/auditory overlay
- SAME overlay shown to ALL group members (inter-subjective consistency)
- DIFFERENT overlay shown to different groups
- **Result:** Shared hallucination (aejectivity via technology)

---

**Example 1: Third Temple / Al-Aqsa**

**Physical reality (objective):**
- Temple Mount platform (stone, ~14 hectares)
- Dome of the Rock (gold dome, octagonal building)
- Al-Aqsa Mosque (southern end)
- **Everyone sees these** (objective reality, universal)

**AR aejectivities (shared hallucinations):**

**Muslims see (Muslim aejectivity):**
- Al-Aqsa Mosque emphasized (AR highlights, enlarges in visual field)
- Islamic architecture overlays (geometric patterns, Arabic calligraphy appears on stones)
- Minaret sounds (call to prayer in AR audio)
- **ALL Muslims see SAME augmentation** (shared hallucination, objective to them)

**Jews see (Jewish aejectivity):**
- Third Temple rebuilt (AR overlay shows Temple structure, as if physically there)
- Hebrew inscriptions (appear on Western Wall, Temple areas)
- Menorah, Temple implements (AR shows sacred objects)
- **ALL Jews see SAME augmentation** (different shared hallucination, objective to them)

**Christians see (Christian aejectivity):**
- Church of Holy Sepulchre emphasized
- Crosses, Christian iconography overlaid
- Biblical scenes (AR shows historical Christian moments)
- **ALL Christians see SAME augmentation** (third shared hallucination, objective to them)

**Same physical space, three different aejectivities (three shared realities coexisting)**

---

**Example 2: Hagia Sophia**

**Physical reality (objective):**
- Byzantine building (537 CE)
- Current state: Mix of Christian and Islamic elements
- Mosaics covered, calligraphy added, then partially restored
- **Everyone sees the physical structure** (objective)

**AR aejectivities:**

**Christians see:**
- Byzantine mosaics RESTORED (AR removes coverings, shows original)
- Christian iconography prominent (crosses, Christ Pantocrator visible)
- Church interior (AR removes Islamic additions, shows Byzantine glory)
- **ALL Christians see Hagia Sophia as church** (shared Christian aejectivity)

**Muslims see:**
- Islamic calligraphy prominent (AR emphasizes Ottoman additions)
- Mihrab, minbar, Islamic architecture highlighted
- Mosque interior (AR minimizes Christian elements)
- **ALL Muslims see Hagia Sophia as mosque** (shared Muslim aejectivity)

**Historians see (secular aejectivity):**
- Timeline overlay (AR shows building through time)
- Both Christian and Islamic periods visible (neutral presentation)
- Architectural evolution (Byzantine → Ottoman → modern)
- **ALL historians see factual history** (shared secular aejectivity)

---

### B. Why AR Aejectivities Are Powerful

**Problem without AR:**
- Muslims and Jews cannot both see "their" site (physical exclusivity)
- Must fight over: Who controls physical space? Whose architecture is built?
- Zero-sum (if mosque built, temple cannot be; if temple built, mosque destroyed)

**Solution with AR aejectivities:**
- Muslims see mosque (AR overlay)
- Jews see temple (AR overlay)
- **Same time, same place, both satisfied** (positive-sum via different shared hallucinations)
- No physical conflict (everyone sees what their group sees)

**The mechanism:**
- AR creates GROUP-SPECIFIC overlays (not individual)
- Each group member sees SAME augmentation (aejectivity, not subjectivity)
- Different groups see DIFFERENT augmentations (multiple aejectivities coexist)

## III. Phases: Packaging Aejectivities

### A. Definition of Phase

**Phase = Sufficient and necessary list of aejectivities active together**

**Components of a phase:**
1. AR overlays (visual/audio shared hallucinations)
2. Legal rules (shared normative reality)
3. Beliefs (shared truths, objective within group)
4. Language (shared communication)
5. Cultural practices (shared behaviors)
6. Social norms (shared expectations)

**All activate/deactivate as package**

---

### B. Why Package Aejectivities?

**Problem with aejectivity mixing:**

**Scenario:** Person at Temple Mount experiences:
- Muslim AR overlay (sees Al-Aqsa)
- BUT secular legal rules (no Sharia)
- BUT English language (not Arabic)
- BUT tourist behavior norms (not Islamic practices)
- **Result:** INCOHERENT (mixed aejectivities conflict, confusing experience)

**Solution with phases:**

**Muslim Phase = Complete package:**
- Aejectivity 1: See Al-Aqsa (AR)
- Aejectivity 2: Sharia applies (legal)
- Aejectivity 3: "Our holy site" (belief)
- Aejectivity 4: Arabic language (communication)
- Aejectivity 5: Islamic practices (behavior)
- **Result:** COHERENT (all aejectivities align, consistent shared reality)

**Phase transition swaps entire package:**
- Muslim Phase (5 Muslim aejectivities) → Jewish Phase (5 Jewish aejectivities)
- Clean swap (no mixing, no confusion)
- Each phase internally consistent

---

### C. Temporal Phasing vs Phase Packages

**Distinction (important):**

**Temporal phasing:**
- Time-sharing (Muslim weeks 1-4, Jewish weeks 5-8, etc.)
- About WHEN each phase is active
- Scheduling mechanism

**Phase (as package of aejectivities):**
- Which aejectivities are bundled together
- About WHAT shared reality is active
- Content definition

**Together:**
- "Muslim Phase active during weeks 1-4" = WHEN (temporal) + WHAT (aejectivity package)
- "Jewish Phase active during weeks 5-8" = different WHEN (temporal) + different WHAT (different aejectivity package)

## IV. Aejectivity Beyond AR (General Concept)

### A. Aejective Truths Everywhere

**Definition:**
- **Aejective truth** = Truth that is objective within group, subjective between groups
- NOT easily verifiable objectively (no universal measurement)
- BUT believed/experienced and shared across subjectivities within group

---

**Examples:**

**Religious aejective truths:**
- "Jesus is risen" (Christian aejectivity)
- "Muhammad is final prophet" (Muslim aejectivity)
- "Torah is divine" (Jewish aejectivity)
- **Each:** Objective to believers, subjective/disputed to non-believers

**Cultural aejective truths:**
- "This is beautiful" (aesthetic aejectivity within culture)
- "This is honorable" (honor aejectivity within warrior culture)
- "This is enlightened" (spiritual aejectivity within ascetic culture)
- **Each:** Objective to culture members, subjective to other cultures

**Historical aejective truths:**
- "Holocaust was 6M" (mainstream aejectivity)
- "Holocaust was <1M" (revisionist aejectivity)
- "British extracted $45T from India" (Indian nationalist aejectivity)
- "British developed India" (British aejectivity)
- **Each:** Objective to that group, disputed by other groups

**Triad aejectivities:**
- German Triad: "We are Germans, this is our land" (objective to members, others might dispute)
- Elizabeth Warren's "Academic Native" Triad: "We are Native American" (objective to members, disputed by Apache Triad)
- **Each Triad:** Has its own aejectivities (shared truths, objective within, contested outside)

---

### B. Why Aejectivity Matters

**Traditional approach:**
- Tries to establish ONE objective truth (universal agreement)
- Fails because: Most truths are aejective (groups disagree)
- Result: Conflict (my aejective truth vs your aejective truth, both claim objectivity)

**Crucible/Jord approach:**
- Recognizes: Most truths are aejective (inter-subjective, not universal)
- Allows: Multiple aejectivities to coexist (different groups, different truths)
- Displays: What each group shares (transparency about aejectivities)
- **Result:** Reduced conflict (accept that groups have different aejective truths, enable coexistence)

---

### C. Sol-Jord and Aejectivities

**How Sol-Jord handles aejectivities:**

**Displays range:**
- Shows: Holocaust mainstream aejectivity (6M) AND revisionist aejectivity (1M) AND others
- Labels: Each as aejectivity (not "truth" vs "false", but "shared by Group A" vs "shared by Group B")
- **No monopoly:** Cannot enforce one aejectivity as universal objective truth

**Enables coexistence:**
- Muslims have their aejectivity (Al-Aqsa is holy)
- Jews have their aejectivity (Temple Mount is holy)
- **Both valid** (within their groups), both displayed, both enabled via AR phases

**Prevents fanaticism:**
- User sees: "My truth is AEJECTIVE (shared within my group), not OBJECTIVE (universal)"
- User sees: Other groups have different aejectivities (also valid within their groups)
- **Result:** Epistemic humility (my group's truth isn't THE truth, just OUR truth)
