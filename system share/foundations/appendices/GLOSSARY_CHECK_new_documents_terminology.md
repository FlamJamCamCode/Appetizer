### A. **Aejectivity / Aejectivities**

**My usage in new documents:**
- "Aejectivities (subjective time experience)" - in phase engine context
- "Each TRUE from their temporal perspective (aejectivity = subjective experience of objective spacetime)"
- Used to describe how different groups experience "ownership" of same space during their phase

**CORRECT DEFINITION (from user):**
- **Aejectivity = SHARED subjective experiencing**
- **Objectivity consistency ONLY between individuals and their overlapping subjectivity**
- NOT just "subjective experience" (that's subjectivity)
- NOT "objective reality" (that's objectivity)
- BUT: **Inter-subjective agreement** (shared experience between subjects)

**ASSESSMENT:** 
- ❌ **INCORRECT understanding** - I defined it as individual subjective experience
- ⚠️ **Partially correct application** - My usage captured the "shared" aspect (different groups experiencing same space differently, but each group SHARES their experience internally)
- ✗ **Missed the key point**: Aejectivity is about AGREEMENT between subjects, not just individual perspective

**FULLY CORRECTED understanding:**

**Aejectivity in AR context:**
- **Muslims at Third Temple**: ALL Muslims SHARE the AR hallucination (see Al-Aqsa Mosque, Islamic architecture) - same augmented reality for all
- **Jews at Third Temple**: ALL Jews SHARE different AR hallucination (see Third Temple, Hebrew inscriptions) - same augmented reality for all Jews
- **Christians at Hagia Sophia**: ALL Christians SHARE AR seeing Byzantine mosaics, icons
- **Muslims at Hagia Sophia**: ALL Muslims SHARE AR seeing Islamic calligraphy, mihrab
- **Each group has SHARED AR overlays** (aejectivity = what the group experiences together via AR)

**Aejectivity beyond AR (general concept):**
- Any shared belief/experience that isn't objectively verifiable but is agreed upon within group
- **Aejective truth** = Truth that is objective WITHIN group, subjective to outsiders
- Examples:
  - Religious belief: "Jesus is risen" (Christians share this, objective to them, subjective to non-Christians)
  - Cultural aesthetic: "This is beautiful" (Ultra-aesthetic Triad shares this, objective to them, subjective to others)
  - Historical narrative: "Holocaust was 6M" (mainstream shares this, objective to them, disputed by others)
  - Group experience: "We experienced discrimination" (group shares this, objective to them, disputed by outsiders)

**The hierarchy:**
- **Subjectivity**: One person's experience (only you)
- **Aejectivity**: Group's shared experience (you + others who agree)
- **Objectivity**: Universal experience (everyone agrees, easily verifiable)

**A Phase = Package of aejectivities:**
- **Definition**: Sufficient and necessary list of which aejectivities are active
- **Muslim Phase at Temple Mount**:
  - Aejectivity 1: AR shows Al-Aqsa (shared hallucination)
  - Aejectivity 2: Islamic law applies (shared rules)
  - Aejectivity 3: "This is our holy site" (shared belief)
  - Aejectivity 4: Arabic language dominates (shared communication)
  - **Package**: All 4 aejectivities together = "Muslim Phase"

**Aejectivity mixing (complex, impractical):**
- Theoretically possible: Share Islamic aejectivity for Temple Mount, share secular aejectivity for marketplace, share personal subjectivity for home
- **Problem**: Confusing (which aejectivity applies when?)
- **Solution**: Phases (package aejectivities by context/location/time)

**Game Masters as "Aejectivity Architects":**
- DESIGN which groups share which experiences (AR overlays, but also broader)
- CREATE inter-subjective consistency (ensure group members agree on shared reality)
- BUILD aejective truths (construct realities that are objective within group, even if subjective outside)
