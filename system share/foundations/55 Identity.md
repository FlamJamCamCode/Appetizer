## The Types of Identity and Their Characteristics

**What it includes**:
- Genetic ancestry (Ashkenazi, Han, Nordic, etc.)

**Why it matters**:

**What it does NOT determine**:

- Your moral worth (genetic quality ≠ moral quality)
