## Why Nothing Gets Solved (When Everything Is Tragic)

**The hiding mechanism**: Each cause of death **hides behind others**.

**Examples**:
- "Why focus on suicide (700k deaths/year) when people die of malaria (600k)?"
- "Why focus on road accidents (1.3M) when people die of cancer (10M)?"
- "Why focus on homicide (400k) when people die of heart disease (18M)?"
- "Why focus on animal slaughter (72B) when humans are dying (60M)?"

**The defense always works**: "Death is everywhere. Such is life. Why single out THIS death when ALL these other deaths exist?"

**Result**: Nothing gets sufficient focus. All causes remain "tragic but inevitable." All hide in the crowd of world tragedy.

## The Solutions That Were Always There (But Hidden)

### **Transportation Accidents** (1.3M deaths/year—largest chunk)

**The problem**: We use "freedom vehicles" (cars) for almost all logistical concerns.

**The observation**: **Anyone who has played any logistical game** (Factorio, Cities: Skylines, transport tycoon) **immediately sees how absolutely idiotic, chaotic, and prone to accidents our logistical system is.**

**Why it's so bad**:
1. **No separation**: Pedestrians, bikes, cars, trucks all mixed on same roads
2. **Human operators**: Fallible, distracted, drunk, reckless, tired
3. **No screening**: "Equal before the law" + melting-pot open societies = ANYONE can drive regardless of skill/responsibility/daemon
4. **Freedom vehicles everywhere**: 2 billion cars globally, 95% sitting idle, causing congestion/accidents when used

**The compounding factor**: "Equal before the law" lets anyone operate dangerous machinery (cars, trucks) without daemon assessment, trust network verification, or exclusion of dangerous persons.

**The solutions** (obvious once accidents are naked):

**Solution 1: Triadization + Exclusion**
- Daemon assessment: Only those proven responsible/skilled/aware can operate vehicles
- Trust networks: Those with accident history excluded (not "punishment" but "you've proven you're dangerous")
- Gradual zoning: Dangerous persons physically separated (cannot access vehicle zones)
- **Result**: Accidents drop 60-80% (just by removing those prone to causing them)

**Solution 2: Automated Logistics**
- Most transport of goods: Could be entirely automated with 0 accidents of human consequence (functionally possible NOW with current tech)
- Autonomous trucks: Run on dedicated lanes/routes, never interact with pedestrians
- Underground logistics tubes: Packages moved automatically (never on surface roads)
- **Result**: 95% of transport accidents eliminated (goods transport is 50% of vehicle-miles, nearly all accidents)

**Solution 3: Separated Zones**
- Pedestrian zones: No vehicles allowed (design prevents accidents, not management)
- Vehicle corridors: No pedestrians possible (grade-separated, enclosed)
- Living areas: Car-free by default (children roam safely, elderly walk without fear)
- **Result**: Remaining accidents drop 80% (pedestrian-vehicle collisions impossible when physically separated)

**Combined effect**: 1.3M road deaths → 50k (96% reduction, from Triadization + automation + separation)
