#### **B. Sensitivity Analysis** (Scenario Planning)
- **Optimistic Case** (30% probability): 30-80× ROI, 45-50% IRR
- **Base Case** (40% probability): 8-40× ROI, 32-38% IRR
- **Moderate Case** (20% probability): 3-15× ROI, 18-25% IRR

**Expected Value Calculation**: 27× ROI | 25% IRR (probability-weighted across all scenarios)

**Why this matters**: Shows investor you've thought through downside cases, not just presenting optimistic scenario.

**The problem investors will immediately flag**:
- 10M animals × €1,000/year × 15 years = €150B
- This is 50-75× the initial investment (IMPOSSIBLE)

**The actual solution** (phased reveal strategy):

- **Year 3** (strategic reveal): Announce once you control 20-30% market → Farmers MUST transition
- **Years 3-10**: Transition support €50-100M/year = €400-700M total
- **Total animal care costs**: €1.4-2.7B over 10 years (NOT €150B)

**Key insight**: Rapid reveal (Year 3, not Year 5-10) is ESSENTIAL for economic viability.

#### **D. Exit Strategy** (How Investors Get Liquidity)

**Option 1: IPO** (Year 20-25)
- Valuation: €24-100B market cap (15-25× P/E for premium food processors)
- Investor returns: **12-40× ROI**

**Option 2: Strategic Acquisition** (Year 15-20)
- Buyers: Nestlé, Unilever, Cargill (post-transition)
- Valuation: €12-45B (10-15× EBITDA)
- Investor returns: **5-18× ROI**

**Option 3: Dividend Recapitalization** (Year 15+)
- Immediate distribution: €3.6-9B (via debt)
- Ongoing dividends: €600M-1.8B/year (50% of net income)
- Investor returns: Return of capital + 15-25% annual yield

**Option 4: Secondary Sale** (Year 10-15)
- Buyers: Sovereign wealth funds, pension funds
- Valuation: €2.4-9.6B (8-12× EBITDA)
- Investor returns: **1-4× ROI** (earlier liquidity)

**Why this matters**: Institutional investors won't commit without clear liquidity path.

- **Strategy**: Regulatory arbitrage + first-mover + force regulatory change
- **Result**: 100-1,000× returns (created $100B+ industries)
- **Similarity**: 60% (legal gray area, first-mover, regulatory disruption)

#### **Precedent 6: OPEC Oil Cartel**
- **Strategy**: Supply restriction → price spike
- **Result**: 10× oil prices (1970s)

**Synthesis**: This strategy combines elements of ALL six proven wealth-creation models. It's not "unprecedented"—it's a synthesis of:

**Each of these alone created billions. This combines ALL of them.**
