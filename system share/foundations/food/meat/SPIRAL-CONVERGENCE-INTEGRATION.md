## Key Concept: The Convergence Principle

### **The Mechanism**

**When you attack death/killing on ALL fronts at once**, whatever doesn't budge stands out as the foremost remaining evil in the world. And so it keeps being unable to maintain "justified" or "outstanding" reasons for existing while others disagree.

**The laser focus**: Having the spiral sweep all aspects of death again and again lets us push with **immense unity force and impact**. Whatever stays outstanding gets **laser focus and massive force** put upon it—**morally, financially, violently**.

### **Why Single-Front Attacks Fail**

- **Veganism alone**: 20% adopt, 80% resist → Industry adapts, continues
- **Lab meat alone**: Takes 30 years to achieve price parity → Industry lobbies for bans
- **Regulation alone**: Industry captures regulators, weakens laws
- **Activism alone**: Exposes horror but provides no alternative
- **Capital alone** (hostile takeover): Industry consolidates other facilities, blocks acquisitions

### **Why Multi-Front Attacks Succeed**

When ALL these approaches attack **simultaneously**:
- Industry must defend everywhere at once (can't)
- Resources spread thin across all fronts
- Morale breaks (no safe position)
- Infrastructure fails (too many pressure points)
- **System collapses** (overwhelmed)
