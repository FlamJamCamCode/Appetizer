## Abstract

**The paradox**: Why would Village Kind (hundreds/thousands of people) willingly work to bootstrap a Tower of Power + Castle that might eventually achieve O Position over them?

**The immediate objection**: "This is insane! You're building the very power that will dominate you! Why help create your own master?"

**The synthesis answer** (from 49 series + 47 series + MIRA + "At Mercy of Gods"):

1. **You're ALREADY at mercy of hidden powers** (49b: foreign Towers, distant states, algorithms, institutions—"gods" you can't see/control). Building aligned Tower = choosing which "god" to be subject to.

2. **Specification tragedy applies** (47c): If you specify "I want safety/prosperity/technology" through democracy/markets → Get shallow version (police state, welfare parasitism, tech monopoles that extract). Tower offers: aligned daemon specification (∞D fulfillment through alignment, not shallow 5D optimization).

3. **Comrade prevention occupies your chance** (49c): Your state/family/neighbors prevent YOU from reaching for power ("anyone but you" pattern). Joining aligned Tower = escaping comrade prevention (Tower pursues power, you benefit from Tower's success because aligned, vs staying where "brothers" prevent you individually).

4. **O Position is coming regardless** (49a): 30,000 Towers will achieve O Position by 2045 (inevitable). Question not "will O Position exist?" but "which O will you be subject to?" Village chooses: aligned ToP (daemon-compatible master) vs random O Position (alien master, hostile extraction).

5. **Trust is bidirectional** (MIRA Ch7): Tower trusts Castle (won't betray), Castle trusts Tower (won't abuse), Village trusts both (will benefit Village even after O Position). All three evaluated on track record, not shared morality—MIRA compatibility determines participation.

**This document explores**: How the three-way trust works, why each kind would voluntarily participate despite power asymmetries, what prevents betrayal, and how this relates to the broader framework.
