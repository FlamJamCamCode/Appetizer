### 1.2 Capitalism's True Genius

**The balance:**

**Why this creates abundance:**

People work far beyond comfort trade-off preference:

**The genius**: Forces humans to create way more than they'd choose (comfort vs effort trade-off ignored because survival at stake).

**The fatal flaw:** Creates abundance through coercion, distributes it to parasites, commands it incompetently.

**Requirement 3: Performance Linkage**
- Cannot be purchased with money
- Cannot be inherited or gifted arbitrarily
- Must be earned through actual contribution in vital domains
- Dynamic ranking (can lose it if outperformed)

### 2.2 The Currency Creation Mechanism

**How currency is "minted":**

```yaml

  step_1: Individual voluntary promise
    - "I promise pregnancy to top 100 cancer researchers"

    - Eternal: Pregnancy completed → Child exists forever
```

**The critical insight:** Currency is created by **saying it is so** (promises), costs nothing to mint (no resources consumed), but becomes extremely valuable because:
- Backed by genuine human willingness
- Scarce by nature (limited wombs, time, attention)
- Cannot be counterfeited (PoP ensures real people, PoU ensures real performance)

**Trust Network enforcement:**
