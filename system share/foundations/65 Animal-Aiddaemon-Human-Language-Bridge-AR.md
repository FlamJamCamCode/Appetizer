### Chapter 2: The Language Gap

**Why animals can't communicate nebulae**:

**Human says "I'm happy"**:

- **Result**: Clear communication

**The gap**: Animals have nebulae but lack encoding scheme (language) to communicate them precisely.

**The recognition**: If we can teach animals to map nebulae to symbols, they can communicate like humans.

**Traditional animal training**:

- **Result**: Pig can communicate nebulae (trigger ❤️ symbol = "I feel loved", trigger ⚠️ symbol = "I'm scared")

- **Result**: Animal comprehends language
