# Chapter 12: Interregnum

## Prologue

Once 0 Position is reached, the eternal stage is set upon the will or mechanism of whatever entity achieves it. Be it dreamlike, indifferent, totalitarian/karmic or nightmarish.

Up until that point: Danger. Turmoil. Escalation. Chaos. And the [many Nightmares occurring all at once](/?f=49a).

The challenge is not only preventing MAD or escalation in the extreme; or only eternal considerations.

The challenge is *also* to bring about acceptable 'optimized steps towards it'.

Which is best enabled by The Crucible, free movement and World Dynamics pushed into MWR 3 instead of 4, by a sufficiently powerful MIRA coalition.

Why? Because a local O may be entirely hellish to some while not to others. Enabling the movement of persons is crucial: The re-association with a more suitable environment, superject or trying at their own setting.

Hence, whatever the Will of the eventual 0 position may be, the Interregnum *ought* adopt The Crucible as a MIRA agreed dealing with the transient state of world dynamics and conflict and human thriving. The unimportant unfolding of the mass of populations, organization and spatial tagging of ownership. Who owns what?

It all becomes *mute, void and null* upon the spearhead reaching of 0 is achieved. All won. All Lost. Becomes unimportant. Whatever state prior to 0 has no weight or consequence beyond 0.

Therefore, The Crucible, is fundamentally an Interregnum state of affairs. To let free the flow of world affairs in tolerable conditions.

Of course, to the economist who sees things in quantity, he would poise and implore, that *it is exactly that mass, institutions and ownership of things* that determines the 0 outcome; to which he is free to explore gaining those things: In The Crucible.

Whereas, those not of those kinds of beliefs, can focus on whatever qualities and quantities of those qualities they find determining in the long haul; in the eternal horizon of things.
