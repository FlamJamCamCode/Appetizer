## General considerations of Human Happiness

Human happiness is *very relatively tied*. That is, the main factor in making a human happy or not, is in the relation to others and the status they sense relative to them. (There are exceptions, though in general it is the case. Almost everyone will at least see it very hard to be ostracized and scorned during their 'coming of age' years / teens)

Usually, persons *hate their faults in an obsessed sense*. That is to say, they fixate their eyes on some narrow sense of worth that they find themselves lacking in. (The opposite persona exists as well, where they fixate their worth in a narrow sense they excel at -- Though they generally do not need help with being happy; therefore not a concern of this discussion.)

Therefore, the general 'function to apply' to humans is to do logistic dampening in the bottom end of traits (raise towards equality or center mass on any negative part to a human that is driving them into sadness / depression), though if it is done at the upper end, then you cause misery for all in an indirect way of never being appreciated for anything excellence (jantelov) and you attempt to guilt trip any reaping of merit (unearned), which sometimes result in equalization of what was gained from applying your merits (theft).

Then, the second part to the equation is to understand high-dimensionality, that is: To understand that there are a million 'games we can play' or 'traits we can have' or 'talents we can rise above with'. "He can do backflips! and that guy can lift heavy stuff! That guy can calculate really fast! And that guy can run fast!" etc. etc. etc.

This high dimensional view and finding environment suitable and complementary of persons they associate with in some purpose, provides a total self-worth comfort. However.............. When the outsider appears and undermines someone completely into uselessness in what merit they thought they had in relation to some purpose and group of persons, then a sense of dread, depression and even suicidality can occur.

From that, it should be clear the function: Equalize bottom to centre mass of all traits. Amplify inequality in its plurality of traits for persons in complementary settings. Avoid bringing into context individuals who undermine the merits of others who have found their thriving setting.
