## Book IV: The Natural Selection System Pattern

### Chapter 10: NSS Across Domains (From Foundations - 33 Document)

**The principle**: Popular becomes weaker, unpopular becomes stronger → prevents monoculture → maintains diversity.

**In games**: Popular mining routes → high competition → low value. Novel routes → no competition → high value. (Perpetual discovery.)

**In research**: Consensus projections → saturated → diminishing returns. Novel projections → unexplored → breakthrough potential. (Wisdom of crowds preserved.)

**In markets**: Popular products → commoditized → low margins. Novel products → unique → high margins. (Innovation rewarded.)

**Automatic growth**: Better performance → more resources → more capacity → even better performance (virtuous cycle for competence).

**Ruthless fairness**: Incompetence hidden behind capital → exposed and starved. Actual performance → rewarded and grown.

---

**The unification**:

**NSS dynamics** (Foundations) + **Performance-based selection** (Conversations) = **Reality-based meritocracy where "what works wins" and capital can't override physics**.

**Not**: One metric rules all (money, status, credentials).

**But**: Each domain has its own selection criteria, diversity flourishes, best solutions in each domain emerge naturally.
