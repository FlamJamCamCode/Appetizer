- **Society loses genius to ad optimization**

- Applied problem-solving → Low status ("Just an engineer")

- Rent: $1,500-3,000

- Transport: $300
- Food: $600

**Vital work pays:** $40k-80k

**Result:** Cannot survive doing vital work. Must choose parasitism.

**Dating market realities:**

**High-status professions for attracting partners:**

**Result:** Choosing vital work = romantic prospects decline.

**For many brilliant people:** This is decisive factor. "I want to cure cancer, but I also want a family. Family requires status/money to attract partner. So parasitism it is."
