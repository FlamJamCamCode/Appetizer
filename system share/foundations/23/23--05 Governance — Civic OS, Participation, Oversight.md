Title: 23--05 Governance — Civic OS, Participation, Oversight

Civic OS (MVP)
- Identity‑anchored scheduling, logging, verification; APIs for ledgers and audits; simple dashboards.

Participation Ballots
- Allocate a bounded tranche (e.g., 20%) of bounty budgets by verified contributor hours (“vote with hands”).

Oversight
- Independent verification boards; observer seats; public dissent notes; random audit lotteries; plain‑language reports.

Resistance/Proximity
- Operate inside constraints (MoUs/legal wrappers) and plan jurisdictional compacts; disclose risks and mitigation plainly.

Sequence Reminder
- Honor → Voluntary uptake → Duty bands; maintain honors throughout.
