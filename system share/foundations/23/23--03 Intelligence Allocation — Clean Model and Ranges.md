Title: 23--03 Intelligence Allocation — Clean Model and Ranges

Problem
- Top intelligence flows to parasitism (adtech/finance), prestige abstraction, and credential gates; vital work starves.

Mechanisms
- Salary/status gradients; drowning costs; credential gatekeeping; meaning‑death cascade; reproductive failure of problem‑solvers.

Clean Ranges (replace point claims)
- Quantity: +3–5× researchers via competence access (not credentials)
- Efficiency: +5–10× via zero‑rent reinvestment and 10–20% productive overhead
- Quality: ×1.5–2 (status inversion, culture; genetic effect uncertain/minor in near term)
- Timing: ×1.3 weighted contribution years (earlier paths)
- Aggregate: 55–150× (central ≈100×). To be measured in pilots.

Training Timelines by Track
- General practice: 2–3y; Specialist: 3–5y; Research frontier: 5–7y.

Selection
- Open trials → supervised practice → outcome‑ranked practice; publish practitioner leaderboards by pattern (blob classes).
