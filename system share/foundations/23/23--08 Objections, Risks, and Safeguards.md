Title: 23--08 Objections, Risks, and Safeguards

Objections (steel‑manned) and Replies
- “Mandated duty reduces supply”: Use duty bands, predictable scheduling, substitutions, exemptions, honors; demonstrate status inversion first.
- “Metrics will be gamed”: Multi‑metric gates; audits; variance publication; negative‑result credit; public reanalysis.
- “Investment chills without IP rents”: Blend charters, bonds, employer pools, prizes; sunset exclusivity; transparent ledgers.
- “Liability exposure”: Corridor wrappers; scoped roles; compassionate‑use lane with explicit consent; incident transparency.
- “Admin bloat”: MVP OS; standard MoUs; overhead target and dashboards; sunset features that don’t prove value.

Risks & Mitigations
- Cultural backlash: Start with honors/ceremonies; clear wins published monthly.
- Equity drift: Publish un/weighted dashboards; coverage floors for underserved wards.
- State resistance: Legal wrappers; operate inside constraints; parallel compacts.
