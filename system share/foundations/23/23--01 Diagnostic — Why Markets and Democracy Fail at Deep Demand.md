Title: 23--01 Diagnostic — Why Markets/Democracy Fail at Deep Demand

Clean Mechanisms
- Time horizon collapse: elections/quarters punish long bets; cures require decades.
- Signal poverty: prices/ballots encode willingness/ability to pay, not survival need.
- Liability‑first walls: certificates gate competence; walls lack doors.
- Attention/mating drag: status to spectacle/wealth; care work under‑rewarded.
- IP/finance rails: monopoly timeframes misalign with cure horizons.

Three Locks
- Narrative: Idol myths overshadow institution builders who move survival curves.
- Money: Drowning floors force inflated wages; cure loses the auction.
- Mating: Status deserts burn out altruists; loneliness blocks persistence.

Legibility Fix (thesis)
- Make deep demand legible as patient‑years saved; ledger outcomes; allocate duty/bounties accordingly.

Ranges & Discipline
- Productive overhead: 10–20% target; forbid “zero diversion” rhetoric.
- Improvement total: 55–150× pending pilots.
