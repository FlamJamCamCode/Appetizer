Title: 23--04 Rails — Duty Bands, Bounties, Corridors, Charters

Duty Bands
- 20–40 days/year for licensed clinicians in pediatric catastrophic domains; substitution markets; exemptions; honors; public completion rolls.

Bounties
- Multi‑metric gates (safety, efficacy, equity, reproducibility); independent verification; random audits; negative‑result credit; clawbacks.

Corridors
- Standard supervised corridors (scope, MoU, training, audits); compassionate‑use corridors (opt‑in, family advocate, separate audits, rapid reporting).

Charters
- Open‑Cure: 100% reinvestment until milestones; sunset exclusivity; public reinvestment ledgers; funding mix allowed (bonds/employer pools/prizes); salary hygiene for force‑funded lines (narrow safety exceptions with sunset).

Overhead Discipline
- Productive overhead target 10–20%; overhead dashboard published quarterly.
