Title: 23--06 Metrics — Patient‑Years, Equity, Productive Overhead

Primary
- Patient‑years saved (risk‑adjusted) with variance and confidence intervals; publish un/weighted equity views.

Secondary
- Duty pledge→schedule→completion; corridor affect/adherence deltas; incident rate; reinvestment ratio; time‑to‑milestone; openness cadence; talent transfer retention.

Overhead
- Productive overhead 10–20% target; dashboards each quarter; treat excursions as design bugs.

Anti‑Gaming
- Multi‑metric gates; negative‑result credit; random audits; independent statisticians; open anonymized aggregates for reanalysis.
