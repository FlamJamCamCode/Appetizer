Title: 23--00 Executive Summary — Commanding Deep Demand

Thesis: Markets and democracies optimize shallow signals; deep demand (keep a child alive) remains unsignaled and thus under‑commanded. Redirect intelligence and power using rails that make deep demand legible, governable, and funded.

Core Claims (clean)
- Command failure: Legible money/ballots misallocate talent/time for survival tasks.
- Moral core: The dying child’s “Save me” specifies civilization’s test; balloons arrive faster than cures.
- Improvement range: 55–150× total (central ≈100×) via: more talent (3–5×), higher efficiency (5–10× with 10–20% overhead), modest quality gains (1.5–2×), earlier contribution (1.3×). Final numbers to be set by pilots.

Rails (build now)
- Duty bands (20–40 days) with substitution markets, exemptions, and honors.
- Multi‑metric bounties (safety, efficacy, equity, reproducibility) with independent verification and random audits.
- Walls‑with‑doors: supervised corridors + compassionate‑use lanes for the dying (opt‑in, separate audits).
- Open‑Cure Charters: 100% reinvestment until milestones; sunset exclusivity; public reinvestment ledgers.
- Civic OS: identity‑anchored scheduling, logging, verification; contributor‑weighted participation ballots.
- Proof‑of‑Care: patient‑years saved ledgers; practitioner‑level outcome rankings; blob‑class discovery.
- Substrate Registry: labs/tools/wards indexed as open substrate with access rules; utilization/outcome tracking.

Sequence
1) Honor/status inversion (ceremonies, heritage access, public rolls)
2) Voluntary uptake and visible completion
3) Duty bands to catch free‑riders (with honors maintained)

Pilots (commit)
- Two hospital ward corridors (affect/adherence deltas; incident rates)
- One metro Duty Exchange (pledge→schedule→complete; clinician NPS)
- One Open‑Cure indication (reinvest ledger; stats sign‑off)
- One public budget ballot (with contributor‑weighted tranche)

Safety/Overhead Discipline
- Productive overhead target band 10–20%; overhead dashboards published quarterly.
- Dual‑lane safety with explicit consent for compassionate‑use; family advocate sign‑off.

Resistance Reality
- Proceed inside constraints (MoUs, legal wrappers) while preparing jurisdictional compacts; disclose risks plainly.

Outcome
- Bind rage to rails; prove with pilots; revise ranges; scale what works.
