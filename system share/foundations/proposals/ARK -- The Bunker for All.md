# Aeternia Resurrection Kingdom (ARK)
*You have grown old. Come to the Aeternia Kingdom and be given new life. New body. New mind. A fresh start.*

**ARK is a project of building a massive protected DOOMSDAY bunker capable of expanding to unlimited space**

## Antarctica as ARK
To convert Antarctica as a nuclear and wind powered facility with two functions:
1. **Protect all from apocalyptic catastrophes and dystopias** instead of the few surviving by escaping earth upon great calamity, then a solution is (also) made for the many, in the form of ARK.
2. **To offer the elixir of youth and Transhumanism** The respawn zone, where after having lived a life, you are allowed to go there, then after some time - if you so choose - be respawned into the general earth under your old or new 'carnal conditions'.


## The Realistic Trajectory Towards
To reach such a scenario, there will have to be a massive build-up of capacity to construct with unobstructed and uninterrupted and unsurveilled command.

Without such conditions, then it simply will never occur.

It is unfeasibly expensive under current conditions and a much too great risk to try.
