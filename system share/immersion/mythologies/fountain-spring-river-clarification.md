# Fountain-Spring-River: The Genetic Stratification Terminology

**Type:** Conceptual Clarification  
**Keywords:** Fountain, Springs, Rivers, Lakes, Homoallelic, Convergent Stable, Reproduction vs Offspring

---

## The Critical Distinction

### What Fountain Actually Means

**FOUNTAIN** (Hyper-Pure, Essentially Clonal):

**Definition**: A population so genetically uniform that reproduction produces **essentially clones**, not offspring with novel combinations.

**The Genetic Requirement**:
- **HOMOALLELIC** on essentially ALL important loci
- Children are **REPRODUCTION** not **OFFSPRING**
- Only surprise is **de novo mutations** (new random mutations)
- No recombination variation (parents too similar genetically)
- **Factory-precision genetics—children nearly identical to parents and each other**

**How to Achieve**:
- 30-50+ generations of ABSOLUTE endogamy
- ZERO external genetics introduced
- Preferential mating within already-similar (assortative mating extreme)
- Small founding population (genetic bottleneck)
- Strong selection PLUS time for fixation
- **Result**: Convergent stable—genetic uniformity achieved

**Example** (Theoretical—rarely achieved):
- Generation 50 of isolated breeding
- All individuals: 6'8" tall (±1 inch—homozygous for all height alleles)
- All individuals: Platinum blonde (100%—fixed allele)
- All individuals: Ice blue eyes (100%—fixed allele)
- All individuals: Same facial structure (homozygous for facial development genes)
- **Children look like siblings even from unrelated parents**—that's how genetically uniform

**This is EXTREMELY RARE**—requires isolation and time most Triads don't have.

## Springs: Strong Selection, Not Clonal

**SPRINGS** (Purest Daemon/Trait Expression):

**Definition**: A population with STRONG selection for specific traits, creating **recognizable type** but NOT clonal uniformity.

**The Genetic Reality**:
- Homozygous on MAJOR traits (height, hair color, eye color)
- Heterozygous on MINOR traits (facial variations, build details, personality)
- Children are **OFFSPRING** (recombination creates variation)
- Surprises: Recombination bringing out different minor trait combinations
- **Recognizable type—"they all look similar"—but not clones**

**How to Achieve**:
- 10-20 generations of strong selection
- Some external genetics allowed (5-10% infusion)
- Assortative mating (similar breeds with similar)
- Moderate population size
- **Result**: Strong trait concentration but not fixation

**Example** (Common in Purity Triads):
- Generation 15 of radiance selection
- Most individuals: 6'4"-6'8" tall (some variation—not fully homozygous)
- Most individuals: Blonde/red hair (90%—strongly selected but not fixed)
- Most individuals: Light eyes (85%—strongly selected)
- Similar facial structure (but variations exist)
- **Children look like they're from same ethnic group**—recognizable type

**This is COMMON**—most successful selection programs reach Springs within 15 generations.

---

## Rivers: Mixing Zones

**RIVERS** (Few Compatible Types Mixing):

**Definition**: Populations formed from mixing of 2-5 different Springs or selected lineages.

**The Genetic Reality**:
- Heterozygous on MOST loci (mixed heritage)
- Children are **OFFSPRING** with high variation
- Recombination creates diverse combinations
- **"They vary a lot but you can see 2-3 source populations"**

**How Forms**:
- Spring A × Spring B mixing zone
- Or: Selected lineage × moderately selected lineage
- Continuous recombination
- **Result**: Variable but patterned

**Example**:
- Spring A: Tall, blonde, blue-eyed (North Germanic type)
- Spring B: Tall, red-haired, green-eyed (Celtic type)
- River (A×B): Children range from blonde to red, blue to green eyes, but all tall and fair
- **Recognizable as "Northern European type" but high internal variation**

---

## Lakes: High Diversity

**LAKES** (Many Types Coexisting):

**Definition**: Populations with many different types present, mixing freely.

**The Genetic Reality**:
- Extreme heterozygosity
- Children highly variable
- No dominant type
- **"Everyone looks different"**

**Example**: Modern cosmopolitan cities—all ancestries mixing.

---

## The Hierarchy of Genetic Uniformity

**From Most Uniform to Most Diverse**:

```
FOUNTAIN (Clonal)
    ↓ Homoallelic on all important loci
    ↓ Reproduction (children = clones essentially)
    ↓ 50+ generations absolute endogamy
    ↓ EXTREMELY RARE
    
SPRINGS (Strong Type)
    ↓ Homozygous on major traits, heterozygous on minor
    ↓ Offspring (recombination variation on minor traits)
    ↓ 10-20 generations strong selection
    ↓ COMMON in Purity Triads
    
RIVERS (Mixed Types)
    ↓ Heterozygous on most loci
    ↓ Offspring (high recombination variation)
    ↓ 2-5 source populations
    ↓ COMMON in mixed zones
    
LAKES (Maximum Diversity)
    ↓ Extreme heterozygosity
    ↓ Offspring (maximum variation)
    ↓ Many source populations
    ↓ COMMON in cosmopolitan areas
```

## The Correction for Historical Entries

**MOST of what I described as "Fountain" was actually SPRINGS**:

**Greenland Giants** (Generation 15):
- Approaching Springs (strong Red Varna concentration)
- NOT Fountain (still too much variation)
- Would need 30+ more generations of absolute endogamy to reach Fountain
- **Springs = strong giant phenotype, recognizable type**
- **Fountain = would be essentially clonal giants (impossible to distinguish)**

**Radiance Triads** (Generation 10):
- Achieved Springs (strong radiance concentration)
- NOT Fountain (recombination still creates variation)
- Children of two "Spring" members still vary (some taller, some shorter, slight hair color differences, etc.)
- **Springs = recognizably radiant type**
- **Fountain = would be indistinguishable (all exactly same height, hair, eyes, face)**

**The True Fountain Requirements**:

To achieve ACTUAL Fountain (homoallelic reproduction):

**1. Extreme Founder Effect**:
- Start with 2-10 individuals (tiny gene pool)
- Or: Start with siblings/very close relatives (already very similar)

**2. Absolute Endogamy**:
- ZERO external genetics for 30-50+ generations
- Only breed within the group
- Preferential mating (most similar × most similar)

**3. Strong Selection**:
- Eliminate deviants (those expressing variation)
- Only allow most-uniform to reproduce
- **Select FOR uniformity itself**

**4. Time**:
- 30-50+ generations minimum
- Possibly 100+ generations for true clonal convergence
- **Patience required**

**5. Small Population**:
- Easier to fix alleles in small group
- 50-200 individuals optimal
- Larger populations harder to make uniform

**The Reality**: 

**TRUE Fountains are so rare that in historical record, possibly NONE exist in humans.**

Even the most isolated populations (island populations, etc.) maintain some variation.

**What DOES exist abundantly**: **Springs**—strongly selected populations with recognizable type but not clonal uniformity.

**Most of my usage of "Fountain" should have been "Springs" or "Approaching Springs."**

## The Proper Usage Going Forward

**Use FOUNTAIN only when**:
- Population essentially clonal
- Children indistinguishable from parents
- Homoallelic on all important genes
- Reproduction not offspring
- **De novo mutations only source of variation**

**Use SPRINGS when**:
- Population strongly selected for traits
- Recognizable type exists
- BUT children still vary (recombination)
- Offspring not reproduction
- **Strong concentration but not clonal**

**Use RIVERS when**:
- 2-5 source populations mixing
- Moderate to high variation
- **Mixing zone**

**Use LAKES when**:
- Many source populations
- High diversity
- **Cosmopolitan mixture**

---

*"Fountain means CLONAL—homoallelic convergent stable where children are reproductions, not offspring. This is RARE, requiring 50+ generations of absolute endogamy with zero infusion. Springs means STRONG TYPE—recognizable consistency from strong selection but still offspring (recombination variation exists). This is COMMON in Purity Triads after 10-20 generations. Most of what appears 'uniform' is Springs, not Fountain. True Fountain is essentially unachieved in human populations—we don't have the patience or isolation to reach it. The terminology matters: Fountain = reproduction (clones). Springs = offspring with strong type. Don't confuse them."*
