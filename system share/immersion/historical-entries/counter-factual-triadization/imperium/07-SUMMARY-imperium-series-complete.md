## The Key Innovations

### 1. The Domain Monopole (NOT Position 0)

**What a monopole is:**
- **Specific ♣ sovereign** dominates one technological domain
- Like Rockefeller's Standard Oil Trust (90% market share, extractive power)
- But earned through innovation (not bought), temporary (20 years, not permanent), beneficially used (infrastructure, not pure extraction)

**Examples:**
- **The Fusion Monopole:** ♣ Schmidt (2035-2055, 20-year lead, extracts 200M person-years/year)
- **The AGI Alignment Monopole:** ♣ Rossi (2037+, only safe framework, extracts 100M person-years/year)
- **The Longevity Monopole:** ♣ Kowalski (2038+, best treatments, extracts 150M person-years/year)

**Why not "Position 0":**
- Position 0 = absolute global supremacy across ALL domains (can impose will on everyone)
- Monopole = domain-specific dominance (controls one technology, earns massive extraction, but others can eventually catch up)
- Imperium collectively might approach Position 0 (45 monopoles combined), but individually = monopoles

**The Trust etymology:**
- "Trust" = fiduciary arrangement (entrusting control to trustees)
- Business Trusts = monopolistic power (Rockefeller, Carnegie, Morgan used trust arrangements to create monopolies)
- Anti-trust = against monopolistic power
- Monopoles = modern Trust equivalents (singular control creating extractive power)
