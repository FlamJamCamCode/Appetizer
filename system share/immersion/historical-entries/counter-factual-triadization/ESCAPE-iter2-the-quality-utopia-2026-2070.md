## Part I: Why Only 3% Escape (The Comrade-Prevention Reality)

From 49c: **"Those calling you 'brother' are your only actual enemies."**

Your state, family, neighbors, comrades, employers, friends—all prevent YOU from organizing power. Foreign enemies cannot prevent you (no jurisdiction, beyond reach). But your brothers can and do.

**The escape barriers** (why 97% trapped):

### Barrier 1: State Prevention (Legal/Violent)

**Strong states prevent successfully** (China, Russia, most Middle East, parts of Asia):

**Example - Shanghai Escape Attempt** (2031):
- 4,000 Chinese professionals coordinate (engineers, scientists, saved €8M each = €32B pooled)
- Plan: Flee to Hong Kong, then to Taiwan/Commune territory
- CCP surveillance detects (AI monitors encrypted communications, identifies organizers, tracks movements)
- **Response** (before they can execute):
  - 200 key organizers arrested (disappeared, interrogated, camps)
  - 3,800 remaining flagged (social credit destroyed, passport invalidated, cannot leave China, border alerts, trapped)
- **Result**: 0 escaped. 4,000 prevented. Savings confiscated. Dreams destroyed.

**China prevention rate: 99.7%** (2028-2070, only 0.3% successfully fled—mostly via dangerous illegal routes, many died trying).

**This is why Chinese Springs don't exist**: Not because Chinese people don't want freedom. Because Chinese state successfully prevents escape.

**Strong state prevention** (2030-2070 success rates):
- China: 99.7% prevented, 0.3% escaped (4.2M out of 1.4B)
- Russia: 99.4% prevented, 0.6% escaped (800K out of 140M)
- Saudi Arabia/Iran/Egypt: 98-99% prevented
- **Total trapped in strong states**: ~2.1B people (prevented from escaping despite wanting to)

### Barrier 2: Family Prevention (Emotional Chains)

**Even in weak states**, families prevent:

**Example - German Engineer** (2032):
- Skilled (PhD physics, €200K saved, qualified for MIRA Spring)
- Wants to join Bergen Spring (applied, accepted, just needs to GO)
- **Family response**:
  - Wife: "I won't go. Leave family/friends/Germany for Norwegian mountains? No. Choose: me or your crazy dreams."
  - Parents: "Abandoning us in old age? Shameful. You're needed here. Stay."
  - Children (ages 8, 11): "Dad, all my friends are here. I don't want to leave. Please don't make us."
- **His choice**: Stay (chose family over utopia, common choice, loving trap)

**Result**: Germany accepted his application (he's qualified), but he never goes (family chains stronger than legal prevention, remains in collapsing Germany).

**Family prevention rate: ~70-80%** (even among qualified, accepted, ABLE to go, most don't—family obligations, emotional chains, love prevents what law cannot).

### Barrier 3: Capital Requirements

**MIRA Spring entry**: €500K-2M (depending on Spring, skills, timing)
**Bunker entry**: €1.5M-10M (depending on bunker quality, location, capacity)

**Who can afford**:
- Spring entry: Top 5-10% wealthy globally (~400-800M people)
- Bunker entry: Top 2-5% wealthy globally (~150-400M people)

**But having wealth ≠ willing to leave**:
- Many wealthy are wealthy BECAUSE of local connections (family business, local real estate, regional networks)
- Leaving = liquidating at loss (real estate €500K might sell for €300K desperate sale)
- Starting over at age 40-50 (career established, social networks, reluctant to abandon)

**Result**: Of 400M who could afford Spring entry, maybe 40M willing to actually leave (10% of qualified, 90% stay despite option).

### Combined Effect: The 3% Ceiling

**Qualified by skills**: 15-20% globally (educated, technical, MIRA-compatible personality)
**Qualified by wealth**: 5-10% globally (can afford entry fees)
**Qualified by both**: 2-3% globally (overlap, ~160-240M people)

**Of those qualified**:
- 30% prevented by strong states (China, Russia, etc.): ~50-70M trapped
- 50% prevented by family/personal ties: ~80-120M choose not to leave
- **20% actually escape**: ~30-50M flee to Springs/bunkers (2026-2070)

**Plus natural growth** (children born in Springs 2030-2070):
- 50M escapees × 2.1 children average × 1.5 Gen 1.5 = +150M born in Springs
- **Total by 2070**: 200M in Springs/bunkers (50M Gen 0 escapees + 150M Gen 1-2 born there)

**This yields 2.4% of 8.4B = 200M** (more realistic than original 1.1% but still selective).

**Adding MIRA subjects** (voluntary adoption):
- States adopt MIRA tech without mass emigration: 1.8B people (21% of global)
- They pay 35% tribute, receive benefits, happiness improves (6.5/10 → 7.2/10)

**Total flourishing**: 200M full utopia (9/10) + 1.8B improved (7.2/10) = 2B total (24%)

**This is more realistic distribution**: 
- 24% flourishing (utopia or improved)
- 76% still trapped (but slowly improving as more adopt MIRA tech)

## Part IV: The Escape Acceleration (Fixed)

- 1.8B under MIRA tech adoption (states paying tribute, subjects benefiting)

**Grand Total Flourishing (2070)**: 
- 26M in Springs/bunkers (full utopia, 9/10 happiness)
- 1.8B MIRA subjects (improved, 7.2/10 happiness)  
- **2B total (24% of 8.4B global population)**

**This is more realistic**:
- Shows S-curve (slow → fast → saturation)
- Demonstrates acceleration from demonstration effect (weak 2027-2032, strong 2040-2055)
- Reaches saturation (geographic + resource limits bind)
- Final 3% in pure utopia is realistic for 49c comrade-prevention strength

## Part V: The Monopole Coordination Protocol (Specified)

**Original problem**: How do 12 monopoles coordinate extraction without overlapping?

**MIRA Coordination Protocol** (established 2036, refined 2040-2045):

### Rule 1: Geographic Domain Allocation

**Each monopole holder claims geographic territory** (where they extract tribute):

**Fusion (Bergen Spring, Schmidt)**:
- Territory: Nordic region (Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Iceland, Baltics = 28M people)
- Extraction: 30% of energy sector value (€8B/year from 28M people)
- Builds: Fusion power plants, trains local engineers, provides infrastructure
- Does NOT extract from: Central Europe (Vienna's territory), or other monopole territories

**AGI (Vienna Spring, Rossi)**:
- Territory: Central Europe (Austria, Germany, Czech, Poland, Hungary = 180M people)
- Extraction: 25% of cognitive labor (education, coordination, analysis = €45B/year value)
- Builds: AI infrastructure, schools, coordination systems
- Does NOT extract from: Nordic (Bergen's territory) or other territories

**Longevity (Copenhagen Spring, Kowalski)**:
- Territory: Western Europe (Denmark, Netherlands, Belgium, France, UK = 240M people)
- Extraction: 35% of healthcare spending (€84B/year from 240M people)
- Builds: Hospitals, longevity clinics, medical training
- Does NOT extract from: Nordic, Central Europe, or other territories

**Others (9 more monopoles)**: Similar territorial divisions (Mediterranean, Eastern Europe, North America zones, South America zones, etc.)

**Total global coverage**: 12 monopoles × average 150M people each = 1.8B people under MIRA extraction (21% of global)

**Non-overlap achieved**: Each person pays tribute to AT MOST 3 monopoles (local monopoles in their region—typically fusion + one specialty + one service = 30% + 15% + 10% = 55% total extraction).

**Compare to dystopia** (49a, 30,000 Towers): Each person under 30-50 Towers simultaneously = 450-750% total demands = impossible = Winnowing.

**MIRA prevents this through geographic coordination**: Your region has 3 monopoles maximum, total extraction 55%, navigable.

### Rule 2: Proceeds Allocation

**MIRA Formula** (transparent, audited quarterly):

**Extraction from subjects** (35% average from 1.8B people):
- Total value: ~€2.8T/year (extracted from subjects globally)

**Allocated to**:
- 60% Infrastructure (€1.68T/year): Build hospitals, schools, power plants, water systems, roads, public goods in subject territories
- 25% Spring operations (€700B/year): Fund Spring members' substrate, research, living expenses
- 10% Defense (€280B/year): MIRA military, Castle maintenance, weapons, protection
- 5% Expansion (€140B/year): Aid to collapsing regions, technology sharing, refugee support

**Verification**:
- Quarterly reports (all Springs publish spending)
- Subject surveys (happiness measured, infrastructure evaluated)
- Cross-inspections (Springs audit each other)
- Enforcement (Springs exceeding personal enrichment flagged, must redistribute or face alliance suspension)

**Why this maintains benevolence**:
- Subjects SEE where proceeds go (hospitals built, schools improved, infrastructure real)
- Benefits exceed extraction (35% out, 40%+ benefits back in different form, net positive)
- Trust builds (transparency + verification + actual delivery = legitimacy)

**This is the CRITICAL mechanism**: Not naive "Springs are good people" but **structural transparency + competitive pressure (bunkers) + alliance enforcement (other Springs) = gentleness sustained through mechanisms not morality.**
