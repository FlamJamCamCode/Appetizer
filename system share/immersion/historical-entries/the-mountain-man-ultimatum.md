# The Mountain Man's Ultimatum: When All Games Are Denied

## The Context (2030)

**The Mountain of a Man speaks**:
- A Celto-Norseman, fiery-blonde-haired, blue-green-gray-eyed, 11'11" tall, descendant of Vikings
- Denied territorial games in Norway
- Denied empty Greenland settlement
- Denied all peaceful options
- Left with only 2.5 choices: Extinction or war, wherein the war variety may function as disgusting degrees of polygamy.

**The setting**: UN General Assembly, after the 92-89 vote denying pioneer petitions

---

## The Speech: "As I Look Ahead, I Am Filled With Foreboding"

### Part I: The Peaceful Petition

**The Mountain Man addresses the assembly** (2030):

> "I stand before you as a remnant. My people once built longships that crossed oceans. We settled Iceland, Greenland, Vinland. We were feared from Lindisfarne to Constantinople. We were the Mountain Men of the North.
> 
> Now we are 0.01% of Scandinavia. Our blonde hair is disappearing. Our blue eyes are becoming rare. Our children will not look like us. Our grandchildren will not exist.
> 
> We came here in peace. We petitioned for games—Viking competitions, Arctic survival challenges. Let us win territory through our ancestral skills. Give us population weighting to make it fair.
> 
> You denied us.
> 
> We petitioned for empty lands—Greenland's frozen coast, the ice and rock where only Vikings would dare to settle. Give us wastelands and we will make them bloom.
> 
> You voted 92-89 against us. Three votes separated us from hope.
> 
> We have asked peacefully. We have petitioned humbly. We have begged for scraps—for games in our homeland, for empty ice in Greenland, for any chance to survive.
> 
> You have given us nothing. You have left us only two choices: Accept extinction quietly, or fight."

### Part II: The Vision of What Comes

**The Mountain Man continues**:

> "I am a reasonable man. I have studied history. I understand the trajectory we are on.
> 
> As I look ahead, I am filled with foreboding. Like the Romans before their fall, I seem to see the future clearly. And it is dark with blood.
> 
> You say 'integrate.' You say 'multicultural society is the future.' You say 'your kind does not deserve separate territory.'
> 
> But I say: You are forcing us toward violence. Not because we want it. But because you have left us no alternative.
> 
> Let me be clear about what will happen if you continue to deny us all peaceful options:

### Part III: The Trajectory

**The Mountain Man describes the future**:

> "**Phase 1** (2030-2035): We accept our situation. We try to 'integrate.' We watch our children marry outside our people. We watch our bloodline disappear. We grow bitter.
> 
> **Phase 2** (2035-2040): Some of us break. Underground movements form. Militias train in secret. We prepare for what we know is coming. Some call us 'extremists.' We call ourselves 'the last generation who will fight.'
> 
> **Phase 3** (2040-2050): Young men—our sons, who will be the last blonde, blue-eyed giants—radicalize. They see no future in your multicultural world. They see only extinction. Some choose to die quietly. Others choose to take enemies with them.
> 
> **Phase 4** (2050-2060): The violence begins. Not mass warfare yet. Individual actions. Terrorism. Assassinations. Sabotage. Your security forces hunt us. We hide in forests, mountains, the Arctic. We become what you called us all along: violent extremists.
> 
> **Phase 5** (2060-2070): The violence escalates. Regional conflicts. Northern territories in flames. You send armies. We fight guerrilla war. Scandinavia burns. Your cities burn. Our villages burn. Everyone burns.
> 
> **Phase 6** (2070+): Total war. MWR Level 4. Millions dead. Civilization destroyed. Everything we both built—gone. The rivers foam with blood. The Tiber, the Rhine, the fjords of Norway—all red with death.
> 
> This is the trajectory. This is what you have chosen by denying us peaceful options.
> 
> I do not want this future. But I see it as clearly as I see you now. And it is coming."

### Part IV: The Mechanism of Radicalization

**The Mountain Man explains how peace becomes war**:

> "You ask: 'How can peaceful petition turn to violence? How can reasonable men become terrorists?'
> 
> I will tell you how. I will describe the process that turns Mountain Men into monsters:
> 
> **The Father** (me, 2030):
> - I am reasonable. I petition peacefully. I accept democracy. I believe in rule of law.
> - You deny me games. You deny me empty lands. You deny me everything.
> - I become bitter, but I remain peaceful. I tell my son: 'Maybe your generation will have better luck.'
> 
> **The Son** (2045):
> - He sees me defeated, broken, watching our people die out.
> - He petitions too. He begs too. You deny him too.
> - He becomes angry. He joins underground groups. He trains. He prepares.
> - I try to calm him. I say: 'Peace is the only way.' He says: 'Peace means extinction. I choose war.'
> 
> **The Grandson** (2060):
> - He never knew a time when we had hope. He is born into bitterness.
> - He sees his father hunted by security forces for 'extremism.'
> - He sees his grandfather's generation extinct. He sees his father's generation dying.
> - He has nothing to lose. He was born to die fighting. And he will.
> 
> This is how you create terrorists. You deny people all peaceful paths. You leave them only extinction or violence. Then you are surprised when they choose violence.
> 
> You will call my grandson a terrorist. I call him a desperate man with no options. You created him by denying me games in 2030."

### Part V: The Alternative You Refuse

**The Mountain Man presents the peaceful path one final time**:

> "There is still time to avoid this future. The path is simple:
> 
> **Option 1: Give us territorial games in Scandinavia**
> - Let us compete through Viking skills
> - Give us population weighting (10,000x for 0.01%)
> - Let us win Norwegian fjords through longship rowing, Arctic survival, Norse crafts
> - If we win, we get sovereign territory
> - If we lose, we accept integration
> - **Result**: 0 deaths, peaceful competition, possible survival
> 
> **Option 2: Give us empty Greenland**
> - East coast, Kaffeklubben to southern fjords
> - Initial funding: $5 billion
> - 10 years of support
> - If we succeed, we get sovereignty
> - If we fail, the land is empty anyway
> - **Result**: 0 deaths, pioneer attempt, possible survival
> 
> **Option 3: Give us any empty land anywhere**
> - Arctic, Antarctic, Siberia (with Russian permission), anywhere harsh
> - We will prove ourselves. We will thrive or die trying.
> - **Result**: 0 deaths, we leave you alone, possible survival
> 
> These are peaceful options. These cost you almost nothing. These save millions of lives.
> 
> But you refuse. You vote 92-89 against us. Three votes from peace. You chose war."

### Part VI: The Ultimatum

**The Mountain Man's final warning**:

> "So I stand here, a Mountain of a Man, 6'8" of Viking blood and muscle, speaking for the last generation of my kind who will petition peacefully.
> 
> I give you this ultimatum. Not as a threat. As a prediction. As a statement of inevitable consequence.
> 
> **If you continue to deny us all peaceful options, this is what will happen**:
> 
> **Within 5 years** (by 2035):
> - Underground movements will form
> - Young men will radicalize
> - Militias will train in secret
> - You will call us 'domestic terrorists'
> - We will call ourselves 'freedom fighters'
> 
> **Within 15 years** (by 2045):
> - Individual violence will begin
> - Assassinations, sabotage, terrorism
> - Your security forces will hunt us
> - We will hide in mountains and forests
> - Scandinavia will become low-intensity conflict zone
> 
> **Within 30 years** (by 2060):
> - Regional conflicts will explode
> - Northern Europe will burn
> - You will send armies
> - We will fight guerrilla war
> - Rivers will run with blood
> 
> **Within 50 years** (by 2080):
> - Total war. MWR Level 4.
> - Millions dead on all sides
> - Civilization destroyed
> - Everything built over 1,000 years—gone
> - The rivers foam with blood, just as the prophet foretold
> 
> This is not what I want. This is what you are forcing upon us by denying all peaceful alternatives.
> 
> I am giving you this warning now, in 2030, while I am still reasonable, still peaceful, still willing to accept games or empty lands.
> 
> My son will not be so reasonable in 2045. My grandson will not negotiate in 2060. They will only fight.
> 
> You are creating them by denying me today."

### Part VII: The Choice Is Yours

**The Mountain Man concludes**:

> "I have laid out the alternatives clearly:
> 
> **Path 1: Give us games**
> - 0 deaths
> - Peaceful competition
> - Possible survival for my people
> - Costs you almost nothing
> 
> **Path 2: Give us empty lands**
> - 0 deaths
> - Pioneer attempt
> - Possible survival for my people
> - Costs you almost nothing
> 
> **Path 3: Deny us everything**
> - Millions of deaths (by 2080)
> - Civilization destroyed
> - My people extinct (but after burning everything down)
> - Costs you everything
> 
> You have chosen Path 3 by voting 92-89 against our pioneer petition. Three votes from peace. You chose war.
> 
> I am telling you now, clearly, what this choice means:
> 
> **You are choosing rivers of blood.**
> 
> Not because I want violence. But because you have mathematically ensured it by denying all peaceful paths.
> 
> When men face extinction, they fight. When people have no hope, they become desperate. When you leave only war as an option, war is what you get.
> 
> I am the last of my kind who will beg. My son will demand. My grandson will simply kill.
> 
> You are creating my grandson's rage by denying my peaceful petition today.
> 
> So I ask you, one final time:
> 
> **Will you give us games? Will you give us empty lands? Will you give us any peaceful path to survival?**
> 
> Or will you deny us, and guarantee that in 50 years, the rivers foam with blood?
> 
> The choice is yours. But the consequences will be on all of us.
> 
> I have spoken. I have warned you. I have petitioned peacefully one final time.
> 
> If you deny us today, do not be surprised when my grandson burns your cities in 2060. You will have forced his hand by denying mine in 2030.
> 
> **The rivers of blood are coming. Not because we want them. But because you have left us no alternative.**"

## The Assembly's Response (2030)

**Silence.**

**Then**: Vote to deny all petitions. Again.

**Then**: Security escorted the Mountain Man from the assembly.

**Then**: His warning was dismissed as "extremist rhetoric."

**Then**: The trajectory began.
