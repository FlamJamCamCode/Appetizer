## Prologue: The Hut-Less World

### The Membrane Collapse

In the modern world (pre-Triadization), **tribal boundaries dissolved**:

**Traditional World**:
- Clear tribes (distinct peoples)
- Membranes (you are this tribe, not that one)
- Huts (physical separation, territorial boundaries)
- Endogamy rules (marry within tribe mostly)
- **Result**: Tribes remained distinct genetically and culturally

**Modern World**:
- No tribes (only "economic zones")
- No membranes (anyone can be anywhere)
- No huts (cities mix all peoples)
- No endogamy (marry whoever, wherever)
- **Result**: Tribes dissolving into economic-zone populations

**The Economic Zone**:

**Definition**: Population defined not by shared genetics, culture, or daemon—but by **shared economic participation in a geographic region**.

**Examples**:
- New York City (economic zone—anyone can live there if they can pay)
- London (economic zone—all backgrounds mixed)
- Los Angeles (economic zone—tribal identity irrelevant)
- **Any modern city**—economic access determines membership, not genetics or culture

**The Consequence**:

**Without tribal membranes**:
- No "we are this people" (just "we live in this economic zone")
- No genetic coherence (all ancestries mixed)
- No cultural unity (all traditions present, none dominant)
- No selection for tribal traits (economic success determines reproduction, not genetic fitness)
- **Result**: Biological entropy—random mixing destroying coherence

**This is "Economic Zone Becoming"—the worst possible form of tribal formation:**

Not: **Conscious selection** (Feminine Becoming—choosing radiance)
Not: **Conquest expansion** (Masculine Becoming—spreading through reach)

But: **Economic constraint + cultural drift** = **dysgenic chaos**
