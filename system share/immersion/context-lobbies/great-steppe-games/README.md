# The Great Steppe Games - Rally Lobbies Collection

## Overview

A comprehensive collection of first-person rally lobby experiences for **The Great Steppe Games** — a speculative alt-world where 4.2 million km² of Russian, Kazakh, and Mongolian territory has been transformed into permanent wargame zones.

**The Core Concept:** Real combat. Real conquest. Real glory. With **respawn islands** instead of permanent death. Fight all day. "Die" gloriously. Respawn in weeks. Return to battle. Repeat eternally.

This is Valhalla made real during life. MoWD (Metagame of World Dynamics) literalized. The warrior's dream fulfilled through technology instead of myth.

---

## The World

### Territory & Scale
- **4.2 million km²** (larger than India)
- **50,000+ active warriors** across 12+ major factions
- **2.4 billion daily viewers** globally (most-watched content in history)
- **$180 billion annual economy** (broadcasting, logistics, land rents)

### The Mechanism
- **Non-lethal combat:** Neural scramblers, blunt weapons, submission/knockout
- **"Death" → Respawn:** Extraction by drone to respawn island → Medical care → 2-3 week recovery → Return to combat
- **Real stakes:** Territory control, wealth, women (voluntary), glory, genetic selection
- **MBC+ protection:** Rule 3 always active (can leave anytime), no permanent harm

### Major Factions
- **Red Banner** (Russian) - Relentless aggression, wolf pack tactics
- **Golden Horde** (Mongolian) - Strategic warfare, intelligence over courage
- **Mongolian Horde** - Mobile raiders, speed and audacity
- **Pashtun Honor Legion** (Afghan) - Pashtunwali code, honor through combat
- **Einherjar Reborn** (Viking descendants) - Valhalla literalized, saga-worthy deeds
- **Valkyrie Legion** (Female warriors) - Merit-based, fighting men as equals
- And more...

## Design Philosophy

### Will-Topological Encoding Per Daemon
- **Warriors** (Russian, Afghan, Viking): Honor, courage, glory language
- **Strategists** (RTS player): APM, optimization, reality-tested strategies
- **Merchants**: Revenue, efficiency, essential asset positioning
- **Healers**: Save count, meaningful work, grateful patients
- **Women** (Conquest prize): Consent, protection, fantasy fulfillment
- **Women** (Valkyrie): Capability, merit, proving strength

### Honest About Biology & Preferences
- Women generally weaker in combat → Valkyrie acknowledges but allows exceptional women
- Conquest fantasy legitimate if consensual + MBC protected
- Different daemon-types need different honor codes (Pashtunwali ≠ Viking ≠ Russian)

### MBC+ Always Active
- **Rule 1:** No permanent harm (respawn system)
- **Rule 2:** No breeding without consent (even conquest prizes choose pregnancy)
- **Rule 3:** Can leave anytime (extraction immediate, no questions)
- **Rule 4:** Information symmetry (all rules disclosed upfront)
