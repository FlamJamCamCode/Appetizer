The Inevitable Four Cards

TL;DR: Trial and Error. Less Talk and more Facilitated Walk.

Imagine a million persons all insisting their point of view is as unique and special as I do mine. Then imagine the effort to understand them even remotely takes reading 15 books. Tiresome. No? Intractible? Why not simply let them walk it.Talk to the universe instead of each other.

If you agree with that consideration. Then you agree with the fundamental motivation of The Crucible. TEST THINGS. Let them BE and BECOME, so you can evaluate them HOLISTICALLY - implicits of it all - instead of having to settle on preference only by explicits and the advertizing proselytizing of it.

◆ Lord of Destruction ◆ 🌿 Natural Selection ⚠ MWR 4 ⚠
